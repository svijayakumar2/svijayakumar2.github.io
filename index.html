I am a second year computer science PhD candidate at Carnegie Mellon working at the intersection of security, graph mining, and machine learning. I am fortunate to be advised by Christos Faloutsos and Matt Fredrikson.

Previously, I was an associate at Goldman Sachs, where I worked for three years. I did my undergraduate at Harvard, where I did my thesis work with Cynthia Dwork and Jim Waldo. I did a joint concentration in computer science and government.

I am supported by the Department of Defense National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate Fellowship.
